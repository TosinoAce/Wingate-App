Accommodation

Bold colours adorn the rooms at Wingate Exotic, allowing you to relax in African style. Enjoy modern furniture and wooden finishes in this polished hotel. Each room is designed with a truly African feel and warm colours to give guests a fresh and new feel as they travel to Wingate Exotic for business or leisure. All rooms offer king sized beds offering complete relaxation in these spacious rooms.

STANDARD ROOM

This comfortable room decorated bold colours. Our standard rooms are equipped with walk in shower and bathtub. Each room is with central air conditioned and has a flat-screen LED TV with 30 satellite channels, cosy king size bed, 24 hours WIFI and spacious wardrobe.

SUPERIOR ROOM

The Deluxe room is finely decorated for your luxurious stay and contains all basic amenities such as Colour LED TV with 30 Satellite channels, cosy king size bed with quality linen, 2 single occasional chairs, dressing mirror, Mini Fridge and it exudes a warm elegance.

EXCUTIVE ROOM

This a cosy furnished space offering a comfortable stay. Each executive suite is aesthetically designed, well ventilated and has a fully central air-conditioned bedroom, bathroom, a living area, best suited for business travellers and tourists.

QUEENABIK ROOM

These stately with posh and bold colours. Our QUEENABIK ROOM is an intimate and charming atmosphere, high quality Wi-Fi, facility, Hi Definition LED TV with 30 Satellite channels, spacious bathroom and guest toilet.

QUEEN ROOM

The QUEEN room have a Pool view which is decorated for your luxurious stay and contains all basic amenities such as Colour LED TV with 30 Satellite channels, cosy king size bed with quality linen, 2 single occasional chairs, dressing mirror, Mini Fridge and a Safe box.

YEMKOL SUITE

A spacious bedroom and living room. Furnishings including a comfortable sofa and a writing desk with internet access capability complete this business environment. In addition, the bathroom is equipped with a bathtub a shower booth to create a more relaxing bath time.

TK SUITE

These stately with posh and bold colours. TK suite is an intimate and charming atmosphere, high quality, Wi-Fi, facility, Hi Definition LED TV with 30 Satellite channels, spacious bathroom.
