Wingate Hotel is equipped to ensure that your meeting, conference, corporate event or product launch is a success. The Auditorium has a large floor space and a height of 6.5 meters, offering choices of daily conference packages tailor-made to suite your needs.

QueenAbik Hall offers a smaller auditorium that is perfect for smaller group of attendees of conferences and seminars. The event hall may be smaller, but uncompromised in quality and equipment.
