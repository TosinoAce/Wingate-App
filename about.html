People & Culture

Ilorin is a cosmopolitan city, welcoming to different people and cultures. Although its indigenous ethnic groups are Fulanis, Nupes, Barubas, and Yoruba, the city and the entire city has become home to many peoples, and Wingate Exotic derived its values of welcoming and accommodating all peoples from the city's heritage. The state is hugely involved in agriculture and the principal crops are Cotton, Cocoa, Coffee, Colanut, Tobacco, Beanseed, and Palm produce - another reason to visit Ilorin and stay with us in order to explore the limitless opportunities these provide.
